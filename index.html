About ScanMitra

ScanMitra is your companion for hassle-free visits to diagnostic centers. It simplifies the process of getting ultrasounds, X-rays, or sonography by connecting patients directly with centers for real-time slot booking.

  • Book slots remotely with your phone
  • Get real-time updates on your appointment status
  • No app downloads needed
  • Simple phone number login
  • Save time, reduce stress, and avoid long waits
